Adobo

Description

Adobo is one of the most loved Filipino dishes of all time. Occasions are not complete without this mouth watering dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Soy sauce
  • Bay leaves
  • Black pepper
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Sutei garlic and onion.
  2. Add Chicken cook until brown.
  3. Add water.
  4. Put in Potatoes and Carrots.
  5. Add soy sauce, Black pepper, Sugar.
  6. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with rice and enjoy.
