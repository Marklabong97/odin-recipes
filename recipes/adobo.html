Adobo

Description

Adobo is one of the most loved Filipino dishes of all time. Occasions are not complete without this mouth watering dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Vinegar
  • Water
  • Black pepper
  • Sugar
  • Bay leaves
  • Oil for suteing

Steps

  1. Sutei onion and garlic
  3. Add chicken and cooked until light brown
  4. Put water and add potatoes and carrots
  5. Add soy sauce, vinegar, black pepper , sugar and bay leaves
  6. Cover and siimmer for 20-25 minutes
