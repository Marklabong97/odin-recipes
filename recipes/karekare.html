Karekare

Description

A unique flavor of pork in delicious peanut sauce topped with vegetables.Usually dipped in shrimp paste.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Puso ng saging
  • String Beans
  • Pechay
  • Eggplant
  • Ground Peanut
  • Water
  • Black pepper
  • Oil for suteing

Steps

  1. Sutei onion and garlic
  3. Add pork and cooked until light brown
  4. Put water and add the vegetables
  5. Add the peanut sauce
  6. Cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes
  7. Serve hot and enjoy
