Sinigang

Description

Sinigang na Baboy is a sour soup dish with a lot of different vegetables. The sour taste comes from tamarind or kamias

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • String beans
  • Gabi
  • Tomatoes
  • Tamarind or Kamias
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Sutei onion and garlic.
  2. Add pork cook until brown.
  3. Add enough water.
  4. Add all the vegetables: string beans, gabi, tomatoes and tamarind.
  5. Season with salt to taste.
  6. Cover and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with rice and enjoy.
